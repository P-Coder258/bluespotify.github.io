Blue Spotify

SP-27

Spring 2025

This CS 4850 course offers a capstone experience for CS majors, preparing them for the workplace or further study. Students will practice project management skills while using modern software tools and technologies. Working in teams, they will develop a project scope, create documentation, implement features, present progress updates, and deliver a final report.

Overview

Spotify has revolutionized the music industry, giving users the freedom to play their favorite songs anytime, anywhere. Taking this inspiration, we want to be able to expand upon and create our own custom take on this popular streaming service. As always, and expected with Spotify, our application will be mobile friendly and cross platform on all devices such as Android, iOS, and Web.
